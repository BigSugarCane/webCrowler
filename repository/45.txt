     <h1 class="cpp-h1">Well Ranked</h1> 
     <section class="text-images" id="s0"> 
      <h2>A Portrait of Success</h2> 
      <p>&nbsp;When it comes to quality education, affordability and career prospects for graduates, Cal Poly Pomona consistently ranks among the best universities in the country. Each ranking category is a snapshot of achievement, but taken collectively, these snapshots provide a picture of a diverse, learn-by-doing university whose students graduate with the skills needed to succeed in a dynamic, evolving work environment.</p> 
      <h2><strong>University</strong></h2> 
      <ul> 
       <li> <p>Cal Poly Pomona is No. 2 among the “<a href="https://www.usnews.com/best-colleges/rankings/regional-universities-west/top-public">Top Public Schools: Regional Universities West</a>” on the U.S. News “2021 Best College Rankings,” published today. The university also rose to <a href="https://www.usnews.com/best-colleges/rankings/regional-universities-west/top-public">No. 10 among all public and private institutions in the west</a>, from No. 14 last year. <br><br>Among schools in the western region, the university rankings included:</p> 
        <ul> 
         <li> <p>3 on the “Most Innovative Schools” list as ranked by higher education leaders</p> </li> 
         <li> <p>5 among “Top Performers in Social Mobility” (tie), rising four places</p> </li> 
         <li> <p>8 in “Best Undergraduate Teaching”</p> </li> 
         <li> <p>11 for “Best Undergraduate Engineering Programs,” in a tie with the U.S. Coast Guard Academy</p> </li> 
        </ul> </li> 
       <li> <p>Cal Poly Pomona is No. 15 nationally and the top California State University in <strong>Money Magazine’s</strong> list of the “<a href="https://money.com/best-colleges/">Best Colleges in America, Ranked by Value 2020</a>,” based on quality, affordability and alumni outcomes. The university also ranked No. 7 among “Best Public Colleges,” No. 2 among “Best Colleges Where More Than Half of Applicants Get In,” No 10 among “Most Transformative Colleges,” and No. 22 among “Best Colleges for Business Majors.</p> </li> 
       <li>Cal Poly Pomona ranked No. 10 in the nation among the universities that help low income students find financial success, according to the “<a href="http://www.socialmobilityindex.org/">2019 Social Mobility Index.</a>” The university is one of only four that has ranked in the ranking’s top 20 for five consecutive years.</li> 
      </ul> 
      <ul> 
       <li><strong>Forbes'</strong> third annual <a href="https://www.forbes.com/value-colleges/list/#tab:rank ">America's Best Value College</a> list ranks Cal Poly Pomona among the best in the nation for a student's return on investment. The university was No. 59 among the 300 schools that made the list.</li> 
      </ul> 
      <ul> 
       <li><strong>PayScale</strong> names Cal Poly Pomona one of the nation's "<a href="http://polycentric.cpp.edu/2018/05/payscale-ranks-cal-poly-pomona-among-best-value-colleges/">Best Value Colleges</a>" in its 2018 College ROI Report. The university is ranked No. 96 nationally based on in-state tuition, moving up from No. 124 in 2017.<br><br></li> 
       <li><strong>Money Magazine</strong> listed Cal Poly Pomona at No. 25 nationally among the 2019 “<a href="http://money.com/money/best-colleges/rankings/best-colleges-for-transfer-students/">Best Colleges for Transfer Students</a>.” The outlet started with educational quality, affordability, and alumni success then looked at four and six-year graduation rates as well as the share of transfer students on campus.<br><br></li> 
       <li>Cal Poly Pomona is among the nation’s leaders for first-year student retention in schools with acceptance rates above 50 percent, according to <strong>The Chronicle of Higher Education</strong>. The university ranked No. 30, with 89 percent of Pomona’s first-year, full-time, undergraduate students in fall 2015 returning to campus for the 2016-2017 academic year</li> 
      </ul> 
      <h2><strong>Program Recognition</strong></h2> 
      <ul> 
       <li>Cal Poly Pomona was ranked one of the “<a href="https://nam03.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fceoworld.biz%2F2019%2F08%2F26%2Fbest-colleges-to-study-architecture-in-los-angeles%2F&amp;data=02%7C01%7Cmmquiroz%40cpp.edu%7C2d3e92368919494ef0f108d73155531c%7C164ba61e39ec4f5d89ffaa1f00a521b4%7C0%7C0%7C637032114844207483&amp;sdata=fizZ8Zz%2Ful04KAPCjjapZj2o0g%2BhjKp64ZAcRrpMy%2B4%3D&amp;reserved=0">Best Colleges to Study Architecture in Los Angeles</a>” by CEO World in August 2019.</li> 
      </ul> 
      <ul> 
       <li><strong>Collins College of Hospitality Management</strong> is ranked No. 19&nbsp;<a href="https://ceoworld.biz/2019/03/04/best-hospitality-and-hotel-management-schools-in-the-world-for-2019/">best hospitality &amp; hotel management school</a> in the world by <strong>CEO World Magazine</strong>.</li> 
      </ul> 
      <ul> 
       <li> <p>The <strong><a href="https://nam03.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.planetizen.com%2Ftopschools&amp;data=02%7C01%7Cmmquiroz%40cpp.edu%7C4feff7266b7d4281a74e08d73bc6c26d%7C164ba61e39ec4f5d89ffaa1f00a521b4%7C0%7C0%7C637043597159075927&amp;sdata=ZK5jAR4wT%2F%2FjA%2BG66VhtN6EgpmoiGySxu0h3zf9DxME%3D&amp;reserved=0">“Planetizen Guide to Urban Planning Programs”</a></strong> ranked the Department of Urban and Regional Planning graduate program, in the College of Environmental Design, among the nation’s best. The program was No. 4 for largest percentage of minority representation, No. 9 for programs without Ph.D. and for suburban program, and No. 14 among top small programs.</p> </li> 
      </ul> 
      <ul> 
       <li>The <a href="https://na01.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.aps.org%2Fprograms%2Feducation%2Fstatistics%2Ftopproducers.cfm&amp;data=02%7C01%7Cmmquiroz%40cpp.edu%7C443750df06db4e348d3e08d6911e7977%7C164ba61e39ec4f5d89ffaa1f00a521b4%7C0%7C0%7C636855957398927982&amp;sdata=HUvtjnVtT5HVU%2FGi%2FLXb0gE0XpQS2d%2F3KMX8Lu6%2BDnI%3D&amp;reserved=0">American Physics Society</a> found that Cal Poly Pomona is among the best in helping students to earn their Bachelor degrees in physics. The university tied for fifth, with the U.S. Naval Academy in the <a href="https://na01.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.aps.org%2Fprograms%2Feducation%2Fstatistics%2Fupload%2F190118-Bachelor-s-in-Physics-at-BS-Institution.pdf&amp;data=02%7C01%7Cmmquiroz%40cpp.edu%7C443750df06db4e348d3e08d6911e7977%7C164ba61e39ec4f5d89ffaa1f00a521b4%7C0%7C0%7C636855957398937991&amp;sdata=QIkJ%2FKSXELxct3hwtqi8AkyyK%2FSh5e%2FroxgedwCj3MQ%3D&amp;reserved=0">number of degrees</a> awarded from 2015-2017. Cal Poly Pomona also came in second, just behind Morehouse College, in physics degrees to <a href="https://na01.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.aps.org%2Fprograms%2Feducation%2Fstatistics%2Fupload%2F190122-URM-BS-at-B.pdf&amp;data=02%7C01%7Cmmquiroz%40cpp.edu%7C443750df06db4e348d3e08d6911e7977%7C164ba61e39ec4f5d89ffaa1f00a521b4%7C0%7C0%7C636855957398947999&amp;sdata=I9sN%2BKjoeOP2I3SSfcE3jdk9Ef9Grxir7ChoepQlHNg%3D&amp;reserved=0">under-represented minority (URM) students</a>.<br><br></li> 
       <li><strong>U.S News</strong>&nbsp;ranked the&nbsp;<a href="https://www.usnews.com/best-colleges/rankings/engineering">Cal Poly Pomona College of Engineering No. 11</a>&nbsp;among the nation’s engineering programs where a doctorate was not offered and No.&nbsp;3 in the state among engineering programs where a doctorate is not offered, and No. 5 in the nation in civil engineering, computer engineering and electrical/electronic/communications engineering. The&nbsp;<strong>American Society of Engineering Education&nbsp;</strong>ranked the College of Engineering No. 19 for percentage of women faculty.<br><br></li> 
       <li>Cal Poly Pomona’s undergraduate <strong>landscape architecture program</strong> was ranked No. 11 in the nation by <strong>DesignIntelligence</strong> in their 2019-2020 list of <a href="https://www.di-rankings.com/">America’s Top-Ranked Architecture and Design Schools</a> among schools with more than 35 graduates. The graduate landscape architecture program was at No. 16. The program was No. 10 in the “top most hired from landscape architecture schools.” The undergraduate program made the top 10 in six of the report’s 12 focus areas, while the graduate program was recognized in four.<br><br></li> 
       <li>Cal Poly Pomona has been recognized among the “<a href="https://na01.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.animationcareerreview.com%2Farticles%2Ftop-10-graphic-design-school-programs-california-2019-college-rankings&amp;data=02%7C01%7Cmmquiroz%40cpp.edu%7C73e81a6877f94cc2926808d69908b8a3%7C164ba61e39ec4f5d89ffaa1f00a521b4%7C0%7C0%7C636864660076465551&amp;sdata=%2FT4cXJf9wmDWLzKGUiT0dxJ5xO6p27krCC2sWrDHdSI%3D&amp;reserved=0">Top 10 Graphic Design Schools and Colleges in California</a>” in 2019 by <strong>Animation Career Review.</strong> The <strong>graphic design program</strong> was ranked at No. 9.<br><br></li> 
       <li>The 2019-2020 <strong>DesignIntelligence</strong> rankings list the <strong>Department of Architecture</strong> at No. 20 among the most admired undergraduate architecture programs in the U.S., No. 34 for top graduate programs and No.4 among the “top 20 most hired from architecture schools.” The department’s undergraduate focus areas made the top 10 in six of the report’s 12 focus areas, led by engineering materials which was ranked No. 5. The graduate programs also made the top 10 in five of the report’s 12 focus areas.<br><br></li> 
       <li><strong>Money Magazine</strong> named Cal Poly Pomona a “<a href="http://time.com/money/best-colleges/rankings/best-colleges-for-business-majors/">2018 Best College for Business Majors</a>.” The university is No. 35 on the list based on estimated price with and without financial aid. The website TopManagementDegrees.com listed Cal Poly Pomona’s MBA program No. 50 among the nation’s “Most Affordable MBA programs,” on list of 50.</li> 
      </ul>

        <ul>

        </ul> </li> 
      </ul> 
      <ul> 
       <li><strong>Diverse Issues in Higher Education&nbsp;</strong>ranked Cal Poly Pomona No. 18 in the nation for Bachelor’s degrees awarded to Hispanic students in all disciplines (based on 2016-17 data). In their expanded online listing for “<a href="https://na01.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=http%3A%2F%2Feditiondigital.net%2Fpublication%2F%3Fi%3D441585%26utm_campaign%3DDIGI1710%2520OCTOBER%25205%25202017%26utm_medium%3Demail%26utm_source%3DEloqua%23%257B%2522issue_id%2522%3A441585%2C%2522page%2522%3A12%257D&amp;data=02%7C01%7C%7C5fba2acf12d840d729fd08d623d1a6bd%7C164ba61e39ec4f5d89ffaa1f00a521b4%7C0%7C0%7C636735780672680809&amp;sdata=MK7vm%2B38mQkeCo6x6v766W2RANT5vHeGX%2F3JampBXzY%3D&amp;reserved=0">The Top of Hispanic Graduates</a><span>,” the university was:</span><br>
