      <h2>Values</h2> 
      <ul type="disc"> 
       <li><strong>Academic Excellence</strong><span>&nbsp;</span>– We demonstrate academic quality, relevance, and excellence through our teaching, learning, scholarship, and creative activities with student centered faculty in an evidence-based culture.</li> 
       <li><strong>Experiential Learning</strong><span>&nbsp;</span>– Our polytechnic identity fosters an integrative approach to education through collaboration, discovery, learn-by-doing, and innovation. Our approach encourages reflection, informed risk-taking, and continuous learning.</li> 
       <li><strong>Student Learning and Success</strong><span>&nbsp;</span>– We are deeply committed to educational experiences and supportive services that engage our students, enhance personal well-being and growth, provide career opportunities, and foster ethical citizenship.</li> 
       <li><strong>Inclusivity</strong><span>&nbsp;</span>– Our diversity across multiple dimensions reflects and enhances our community. We are welcoming and respectful, and we value diversity.</li> 
       <li><strong>Community Engagement</strong><span>&nbsp;</span>– We nurture mutually beneficial and meaningful relationships with community partners and stakeholders.</li> 
       <li><strong>Social and Environmental Responsibility</strong><span>&nbsp;</span>– As global citizens, our individual and collective actions reflect our commitment to one another, society, and the environment.</li> 
      </ul> 
      <h2>Strategic Initiatives</h2> 
      <h3>Deliver quality programs that promote integrative learning, discovery and creativity</h3> 
      <ul> 
       <li>As an expression of our polytechnic identity, all students at Cal Poly Pomona will engage in experiential applied learning across all disciplines.&nbsp;</li> 
       <li>Expand opportunities for students to experience the distinguishing hallmarks of a Cal Poly Pomona education: integrative learning, discovery, and creativity.&nbsp;</li> 
       <li>Establish centers of excellence that will capitalize on our polytechnic identity, strengths and synergistic opportunities for discovery, innovation, and creative expression.&nbsp;</li> 
       <li>Revitalize the General Education program by reimagining the integration of the liberal arts and sciences within the context of our polytechnic identity.</li> 
      </ul> 
      <h3>Enhance student learning, development, and success</h3> 
      <ul> 
       <li>Re-envision the co-curricular experience of students as they move in, through and beyond the University community.&nbsp;</li> 
       <li>Design effective strategies that optimize the retention, persistence and achievement of students from historically underserved communities.&nbsp;</li> 
       <li>Develop innovative strategies to increase the graduation rates of all students.&nbsp;</li> 
       <li>Expand the digital student experience to provide timely and effective services that support students and their success.&nbsp;</li> 
       <li>Encourage widespread student engagement in experiences that foster strong and enduring relationships and a sense of belonging in a vibrant campus community.&nbsp;</li> 
       <li>Create a culture that promotes wellbeing and resiliency.</li> 
      </ul> 
      <h3>Prepare our students for the future of work and civic engagement</h3> 
      <ul> 
       <li>Implement a plan for student success that spans preadmission to one-year post-graduation.&nbsp;</li> 
       <li>Develop a career readiness model that provides students with early and on-going opportunities to engage with alumni, the community, and industry leaders.&nbsp;</li> 
       <li>Create a civic engagement i-Lab to address and solve community concerns and regional needs.</li>
