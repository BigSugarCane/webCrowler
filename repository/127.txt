     <h1 class="cpp-h1">Nearby Attractions</h1> 
     <section class="text-images" id="s0"> 
      <h2>On Campus</h2> 
      <p><strong><span>AGRIscapes </span></strong><br>Located on 40 acres at Cal Poly Pomona,&nbsp; <a href="https://www.cpp.edu/~agriscapes/" title="Cal Poly Pomona AGRIscapes">AGRIscapes</a>&nbsp;showcases farming and urban landscaping practices that are environmentally beneficial, economically viable, and technologically sound.</p> 
      <p><span><strong>Rainbird BioTrek</strong>&nbsp;</span> <br> <a href="http://www.cpp.edu/~biotrek/" title="Cal Poly Pomona BioTrek">Rain Bird BioTrek</a>&nbsp;is an educational enterprise of the Biological Sciences Department, featuring a tropical rainforest, greenhouses, gardens and labs.</p> 
      <p><span><strong>John T. Lyle Center for Regenerative Studies</strong>&nbsp;</span> <br>The&nbsp;<a href="http://www.cpp.edu/~crs/" title="Cal Poly Pomona John T. Lyle Center for Regenerative Studies">John T. Lyle Center for Regenerative Studies</a> <span>&nbsp;</span>is an interdisciplinary demonstration and research center focused on regenerative and sustainable systems.</p> 
      <p><span><strong>W.K. Kellogg Arabian Horse Library</strong> &nbsp;</span> <br>The <a href="https://www.cpp.edu/~library/kellogg-arabian-horse-library/index.shtml">W.K. Kellogg Arabian Horse Library</a>&nbsp;is located inside the first floor of the <span>&nbsp;</span> <a href="http://www.cpp.edu/library">University Library</a>.&nbsp;It is one of the world’s largest public collections of Arabian horse materials. &nbsp;</p> 
      <p><span><strong>Kellogg House Pomona</strong>&nbsp;</span> <br>Before it became&nbsp;<a href="http://www.kellogghousepomona.com/" title="Cal Poly Pomona Kellogg House Pomona">Kellogg House Pomona</a>, the mansion was once W.K. Kellogg's West Coast home. The 8,777–square-foot house was designed by renowned architect Myron Hunt, whose projects included the Pasadena Rose Bowl and the Huntington Library. Charles Gibbs Adams, a distinguished landscape architect who worked on the Hearst Castle gardens in San Simeon, designed the lush and colorful surroundings.</p> 
      <p><span><strong>Theater Mainstage</strong>&nbsp;</span> <br>Visit the <a href="http://www.cpp.edu/~class/theatre-new-dance/" title="Cal Poly Pomona Theater Department">theatre department's homepage</a>&nbsp;for information on current and upcoming shows.</p> 
      <p><span><strong>Music Peformances</strong>&nbsp;</span> <br>Vist the <a href="http://www.cpp.edu/~class/music/" title="Cal Poly Pomona Music Department">music department's homepage</a>&nbsp;for information on current and upcoming performances.</p> 
      <p><span><strong>Rose Float</strong></span> <br>Since 1949, the Cal Poly Universities in Pomona and San Luis Obispo, have worked together to select the design, build and decorate a float for the Rose Parade. “A New Leaf” won the Founders’ Trophy in the 2017 Rose Parade. The team has earned 57 awards at the parade.&nbsp;Visit the&nbsp;<a href="http://www.rosefloat.org/" title="Cal Poly Pomona Rose Float">Rose Float homepage</a>&nbsp;for information on current and past floats.</p> 
      <p><span><strong>University Library</strong> </span><br>The&nbsp;<a href="http://www.cpp.edu/library">University Library</a>&nbsp;is a place where discovery, creativity and collaboration are constantly taking place. Check out the&nbsp;<a href="http://www.cpp.edu/~library/about/spotlight.shtml">library calendar</a>&nbsp;to see upcoming events.</p> 
      <p><span><strong>W.K. Kellogg Arabian Horse Center</strong> </span><br>The&nbsp;<a href="http://www.wkkelloggarabianhorsecenter.com/" title="W.K. Kellogg Arabian Horse Center">W.K. Kellogg Arabian Horse Center</a> <span>&nbsp;</span>promotes interest in the Arabian breed, demonstrates the horse’s versatility, beauty and intelligence, and also offers valuable experiences for students. The center hosts the Sunday Arabian Horse Shows, the oldest tradition on campus, on the first Sunday of the month from October through May.</p> 
      <p><strong><span>W. Keith &amp; Janet Kellogg Art Gallery and the Huntley Gallery</span> <br> </strong>The two galleries offer diverse and multicultural exhibits. Please visit the&nbsp;art gallery's homepage&nbsp;for information on current and up-and-coming shows and when the gallery is open.</p>
